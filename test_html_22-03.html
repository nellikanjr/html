Emoji

This is tile Emoji are ideograms and smileys used in electronic messages and web pages

In 1999 Shigetaka Kurita created the first widely-used set of emoji. He was part of the team working on NTT DoCoMo's i-mode mobile Internet platform. ... Kurita created the first 180 emoji based on the expressions that he observed people making and other things in the city.
